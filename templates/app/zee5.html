ZEE5 is an Indian on-demand Internet streaming media provider run by Essel Group via its subsidiary Zee Entertainment Enterprises. It was launched in India on 14 February 2018 with content in 12 languages.

The ZEE5 mobile app is available on Web, Android, iOS, Smart TVs, among other devices.
